## 1. Personal data protection

The processing that VALLAX performs on personal data obtained from users through this website complies with Organic Law 3/2018 and, supplementarily, with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 on the protection of natural persons.

## 2. Data controller

- **Controller:** Vallax (brand under development)
- **Contact email:** [vallaxdev@gmail.com](mailto:vallaxdev@gmail.com)

## 3. Purpose of the processing

Data are only used to reply to user inquiries, manage contact requests, and, where appropriate, send quotes or offers related to Vallax’s services.

## 4. Data sharing

Data will not be shared with third parties unless legally required or expressly authorized. They may only be communicated to processors who have signed agreements guaranteeing equivalent security measures.

## 5. Principles applied

- **Accuracy:** the user guarantees that the information provided is truthful and precise.
- **Data minimization:** only the data strictly necessary to initiate contact (name and contact details) are requested.
- **Lawfulness and transparency:** processing is based on the user’s consent when submitting a request.
- **Storage limitation:** data are kept only as long as necessary unless deletion is requested.
- **Confidentiality and integrity:** security measures are in place to preserve confidentiality.
- **Minors:** the service is intended for adults only.

## 6. Exercising your rights

You may exercise your rights under data-protection law (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability) through the contact channels provided on this website. These rights may only be exercised by the data subject. VALLAX will respond within ten days. You may also contact the Spanish Data Protection Agency if you deem it necessary.

## 7. Right to erasure

When data are no longer necessary for the purposes for which they were collected, or the data subject requests deletion, they will be erased unless there is a legal obligation to retain them.

## 8. Security measures

VALLAX maintains technical measures to mitigate service-related risks and guarantee the integrity of personal data. Any breach affecting the data will be reported to the Spanish Data Protection Agency and to the affected parties within 72 hours.

## 9. Changes to this policy

VALLAX may modify this policy whenever required or due to regulatory updates. Changes will be published on the website at least five days before they take effect.
